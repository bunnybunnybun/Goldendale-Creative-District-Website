We are the Goldendale Creative District Steering Committee

Mission

Goldendale Creative District fosters economic development through the arts, partnerships and creative culture.

Vision

We aim to stimulate a welcoming, thriving and vibrant Goldendale, by embracing our past and welcoming the future.

Values

  • Honor diverse cultural identities;
  • Provide equitable access to artistic opportunities;
  • Encourage community service;
  • Promote community and individual well-being.
